LLM Geocoding

I was playing around with Google's Geocoding API when I thought to plug in an address to o1 for shits and giggles. Imagine my surprise when it actually got it right down to the first 3 decimal places (precision of within hundreds of meters)! Considering the fact that it got in the near vicinity without any internet access at all, that's pretty impressive.
